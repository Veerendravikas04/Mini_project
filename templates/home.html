About Connect 4

Connect 4 is a timeless two-player strategy game where players alternately drop colored discs into a vertical grid. The objective is to align four discs in a row—vertically, horizontally, or diagonally—before your opponent does.

This digital version lets you play against a smart AI with a modern interface. Whether you're sharpening your strategic skills or playing for fun, Connect 4 offers a simple yet challenging experience for all ages.

How to Play Connect 4

  • Start the Game: The game begins with the player making the first move by clicking on any column to drop a disc.
  • Disc Drops Automatically: The disc will fall to the lowest available position in the selected column.
  • AI Takes Its Turn: After the player’s move, the AI instantly plays its turn by placing its disc in a strategic spot.
  • Alternate Turns: The player and AI continue to take turns until the board is full or one of them connects four discs in a row.
  • Win Condition: The game ends when either the player or the AI forms a consecutive line of four discs vertically, horizontally, or diagonally.
  • Game Over: If the board fills up with no winner, the game results in a draw.
